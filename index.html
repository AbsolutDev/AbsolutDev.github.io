I am a self-taught web developer based in London,
with a technical background and vast
Project and IT Service Management experience
Originally from Bucharest, Romania, my interest in web development started in the early 2000s playing around with HTML4, CSS and even a bit of JavaScript. However, I went on a completely different journey in 2003 when I joined a global company that offered me the right environment and opportunities to grow and develop into becoming an experienced IT professional.
Having had roles in various IT areas, from Technical Consultancy and Business Analysis to Project and IT Service Management, I had the chance to meet and work with wonderful people, successfully collaborating with stakeholders at any levels, from IT engineers to top team business managers.
One of the roles I have accepted has required me to relocate to London, where I currently live with my wife and 11 years old daughter.
Fast forward to mid-2019, I have decided that after 15+ years it is time to move on, and so I have accepted voluntary redundancy. The COVID-19 pandemic put on hold my plans of returning to work. However, it gave me the chance to spend time homeschooling my daughter and also explore different areas in technology, from cloud architecture and containerization, to network management and the Internet of Things. This, together with the abundance of tools and courses available online nowadays, led me to rediscovering my passion for coding and for designing and creating things that have real purpose.
I am quietly confident, naturally curious and detail-oriented, always striving to improve my skills and knowledge, taking it one step at a time. I love the process of coding things from scratch and bringing ideas to life on the computer, which is what excites me most about starting a career in software development.
I have a customer-focused approach and have been recognized as a problem solver, one of my greatest strenghts being the ability to remain calm and focused even in pressing circumstances.
While I value the autonomy and focus that come with working independently, I also thrive in collaborative environment and enjoy being part of teams of any size. I believe that effective teamwork is essential to achieving great results and I am always looking for ways to improve collaboration and communication within a team.
When I am not in front of my laptop, you'll find me listening to music, watching a good movie, cooking, gardening, outside in a park with my daughter or exploring the cliffs in Cornwall.
I am eager to apply my knowledge and experience as a Junior Front End Developer, and I am excited about the opportunity to join a team that shares my passion for software development. I am constantly seeking to expand my skillset and knowledge, with the goal of ultimately becoming a Full Stack Engineer or Team Lead.
