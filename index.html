About Me
Detail oriented, naturally curious and quietly confident, I love the process of coding things from scratch and bringing ideas to life on the computer, which is what excites me most about a career in software development.
Originally from Bucharest, Romania, my interest in web development started in the early 2000s playing around with HTML4, CSS and even a bit of JavaScript.
However, I have pursued a different path when I took the opportunity of joining a respected global company that has enabled me to grow into an experienced IT professional.
Having had roles in various IT areas from Technical Consultancy and Business Analysis to Project and IT Service Management, I thrive in a tech-centric setting while being able to successfully collaborate with business managers across all levels.
Fast forward to today, after exploring different areas in technology from cloud architecture and containerization, to network management and the Internet of Things, I have rediscovered my passion for coding and for designing and creating things that have real purpose.
Currently living in London with my wife and 11 year old daughter, when I am not in front of my computer you'll find me listening to music, watching a good movie, cycling or exploring the rugged cliffs of Cornwall.
